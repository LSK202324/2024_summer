Directions

Incheon Int'l Airport ↔ Kyung Hee University Seoul Campus

#1 By Subway (Incheon Int'l Airport → Seoul Station → Hoegi Station)
  1. Getting to Airport Rail Station at the Airport (Incheon Int'l Airport → Seoul Station)
    • 1F of the passenger terminal → B1F of the passenger terminal → Transportation Center → Incheon Int'l Airport Station
    • Take AREX (Airport Railroad train) at Incheon Int'l Airport Station
    • Tickets can be purchased from the ticket machines near the entrance to the AREX platform or from the service counters (KRW 18,000). When you purchase the ticket, you have to select "Hoegi station" as your final destination (the ticket you purchased can be used for the transfer to the subway). Otherwise, you need to buy another ticket for the subway.
  2. Transfer from Seoul Station (AREX) to Seoul Station (subway line 1)
    • Follow the guide to the subway line 1
    • Use your ticket for the transfer
  3. Seoul Station (subway line 1) → Hoegi Station
    • Line 1 has two directions:
      • direction to Incheon / Cheonan / Suwon (next station: 남영 Namyeong)
      • direction to Cheongnyangni / Uijeongbu / Kwangwoon Univ. / Dongducheon / Yeoncheon (next station: 시청 City Hall)
    • Take a train bound for Uijeongbu / Kwnagwoon Univ. / Dongducheon / Yeoncheon (next station: 시청 City hall)
      If you take trains for Cheongnyangni or Dongmyo, you have to make an additional transfer.
  4. Take mini bus 01 to Kyung Hee University (the last station of the mini bus) from Hoegi Station exit 1 (KRW 1,400; approx. USD 1)

#2 By Airport Limousine bus (Incheon Int'l Airport → Cheongnyangni Station → Hoegi Station)
  1. Take airport bus 6002 from Incheon Int'l Airport bus stop 12A to the Cheongnyangni Station (the last stop).
    For additional information: Airport Limousine
  2. Take taxi to Kyung Hee University from the Cheongnyangni Station, probably there is also option for the subway but the cost is almost same (KRW 7,000; approx. USD 5)
  3. Show the taxi driver this message.
    (KOREAN: 경희대학교 서울캠퍼스 정문으로 가주세요. ENGLISH: Kyung Hee University at Seoul, please.)

#3 By taxi
  • Alternatively, participants can take a regular (white, silver-gray, or orange sedan) taxi or a deluxe (black sedan) taxi to Kyung Hee University. To take a taxi, please use the taxi stop in front of the passenger terminal of Gimpo Airport.
  • Show the taxi driver this message.
    (KOREAN: 경희대학교 서울캠퍼스 정문으로 가주세요. ENGLISH: Kyung Hee University at Seoul, please.)


Gimpo Int'l Airport ↔ Kyung Hee University Seoul Campus

#1 By Subway (Gimpo Int'l Airport → Seoul Station → Hoegi Station)
  1. Purchase an one-time subway ticket. (Final destination: "Hoegi station" (KRW 5,050; approx. USD 3)
    Or, you may purchase a Public Transportation card (T-money; Cashbee - you can get one at a convenience store) for multiple rides. You have to pay for a card itself first, and (re)load before use. (recommended valance: at least KRW 15,000)
  2. Take Airport Railroad at Gimpo Int'l Airport Station (next station: 마곡나루 Magongnaru) and take off the Train at Seoul Station (the last station).
  3. Transfer to subway line 1 to Hoegi Station; Do NOT go outside the gate.
  4. Line 1 has two directions:
    • direction to Incheon / Cheonan / Suwon (next station: 남영 Namyeong)
    • direction to Cheongnyangni / Uijeongbu / Kwangwoon Univ. / Dongducheon / Yeoncheon (next station: 시청 City Hall)
  5. Take Bus Dongdaemun 01 (동대문01) to Kyung Hee University (the last station) from Hoegi Station exit 1 (KRW1,400; approx. USD 1).

#2 By taxi
  • Alternatively, participants can take a regular (white, silver-gray, or orange sedan) taxi or a deluxe (black sedan) taxi to Kyung Hee University. To take a taxi, please use the taxi stop in front of the passenger terminal of Incheon Airport.
  • Show the taxi driver this message.
    (KOREAN: 경희대학교 서울캠퍼스 정문으로 가주세요. ENGLISH: Kyung Hee University at Seoul, please.)
